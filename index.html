BITSAPIENS

I was a biological machine born in 1980, with time, I became a machine of questions — more intense each year. Since 2013, I have been obsessed with the arrival of the other machines. Now, the question is: What will the first post-biological life look like? This site is the universe I am creating to answer that. Art, tech, and ethics will not be enough.
