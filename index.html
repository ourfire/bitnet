TRUE LOVE

An experimental crypto artwork consisting of an algorithm that seeks to generate all possible Ethereum wallets in search of the ones containing "Dead Ringers." The search itself is the love story, the work of art, and a tribute to what makes us human - our absurd drive to search for things we may never find.
